I have always loved photography. When I was younger, I would

always take my Nana's camera and just go at it. I mean, I took pictures

of everything I saw or that interested me.

Sometimes I see the most beautiful picture in my eyes, but when I actually try

to take the picture it's nothing like what was in my head. In my head it was

brighter and more contrast than just on a smartphone. My photography differs from what

I think would be a great picture.
